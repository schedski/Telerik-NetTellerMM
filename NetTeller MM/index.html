Help

Login

Logging in

The first time you log into the MM 5.2.2 app, you will be required to enter the customer number and password you use to log into Internet Banking. Once you have successfully logged in, you will be given the option to create a PIN for quicker login in the future.

Setting up a PIN

When setting a PIN for the first time, you will need to enter a 4-digit number and re-enter it to confirm. The next time you log in to the app, you will be prompted for your PIN instead of your customer number and password.

If you forget your PIN, you can tap the 'Forgot PIN' button and login with your customer number and password instead. If you still have problems logging in, you will need to contact the call centre, who will help reset your password.

Setting a PIN

If you chose not to set up a PIN when you first logged in, you can do so at a later date by going to the Settings tab and tapping the 'Set up your PIN' option. Note, this option is disabled if a PIN has already been set up on your device for a different customer number. See the 'Deregistering a device' below if you wish to change this.

Changing your PIN

You can change your PIN at any time once you are logged in. Go to the Settings tab and select the 'Change your PIN' option.

Deregistering a device

You can choose to disable PIN login on your device altogether if you no longer wish to use this feature. Go to the Settings tab and tap the 'Deregister Device' option. This will revert the login method back to customer number and password. Note that if you choose to setup the deice for PIN login again in the future, the original PIN will be used. You can however, choose to change your PIN as explained in 'Changing your PIN' above.

Note: if you do not see the 'Deregister Device' option in Settings, there is no PIN currently set up for the logged in customer number.

Accounts and Balances

Viewing Account Balances

When you have successfully logged in, your accounts will be listed, along with their current and available balances. Tap an account to view the most recent transactions for that account.

You can view transactions from the last 30 days in the app, although this may vary depending on your institution and the type of account being viewed. For a more detailed list of transactions, please log into Internet Banking.

Payments

Payments

You can perform the following tasks on this tab:

- Transfer money between your accounts and/or those that you have authority to debit and/or credit from.

- Pay to accounts at other institutions using a valid BSB and account number.

- Make BPAY payments using a valid biller code and customer reference number (CRN).

Note: depending on how your accounts are setup, you may not be able to pay to new payees or BPAY billers. See the Contacts section for more details on adding new payees and billers.

Transfer

Tap the Payments tab and choose the 'Transfer' option. Select an account to transfer money from and an account to transfer from. Enter the amount and a short description and tap Submit.

Pay

Tap the Payments tab and choose the 'Pay' option. Select the account from which you want to pay and then choose the contact you want to pay. You will be taken to the Contacts page where you can either choose an existing saved payee or add a new one (if this option is available).

Note: if you have Two-Factor Security enabled, you will be required to enter a One Time Password in order to add a new payee.

BPAY

Tap the Payments tab and choose the 'BPAY' option. Select the account from which you want to pay and then choose the contact you want to pay. You will be taken to the Contacts page where you can either choose an existing saved biller or add a new one (if this option is available).

Note: if you have Two-Factor Security enabled, you will be required to enter a One Time Password in order to add a new biller.

Contacts

Contacts

You can manage an address book of payees and BPAY billers from within the app to make it easier to pay accounts and bills in the future.

Tap the Contacts tab to view any existing payees or billers that may have aleady been set up on your account in Internet Banking. If you do not have any contacts, you can add them from within the app (if this option is available).

Note: if you have Two-Factor Security enabled, you will be required to enter a One Time Password via an appropriate security device (SMS or VASCO) when adding new payees and billers.

Managing Contacts

You can edit the nickname of saved payees and billers or delete them from your Contacts altogether. Tap the gear icon alongside the relevant contact and choose modify or delete. The contact will be updated or removed in Internet Banking as well.

Note: for security reasons you cannot change the BSB or account number for payees, or the biller code or CRN for billers. If you need to modify these details, delete the payee or biller and add the new details.

Security

Two-Factor Security

Two-Factor Security, also known as Two-Factor Authentication, or 2FA, is an additional layer of security that protects your account from fraudulent use. If your bank has enabled it, or you are registered for 2FA, you will be required to enter a One Time Password that has been either sent to your registered mobile phone number or generated by a token device that you own when adding new payees or billers.
